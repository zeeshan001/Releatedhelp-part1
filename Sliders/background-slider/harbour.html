Welcome to Harbour Towne Park

Single Family Homes from the $190's

Located just off Kyle's Station Road Harbour Towne Park is an intimate community with desirable home sites including cul-de-sac and wooded areas. It is nestled in a very quiet setting in Liberty Township, yet convenient to amultitude of shopping areas and restaurants. Harbour Towne Park offers a wide variety of ranch, two story, and multi-level home designs to choose from and we're happy to work with each newhomeowner to customize their future dream home to meet their needs.Standard features include 1st floor brick wrap, full basements, landscapingpackage, 20 year Dry Basement Warranty, 10 year RWC, third-party EnergyEfficiency Certification and more!
